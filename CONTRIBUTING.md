## Ways to Contribute

### 1. Suggest a Tool (Easiest)

[Open an issue](https://github.com/GINIUS123/EUfirst/issues/new?template=suggest-tool.md) and include:

- **Tool name**
- **Website URL**
- **Country of HQ**
- **Category** (LLMs, Cloud, Dev Tools, Productivity, etc.)
- **What it does** (1-2 sentences)

## Disclosure

If you are affiliated with a tool you're suggesting (employee, founder, investor), **disclose it** in your issue or PR description. We welcome submissions from tool makers â€” we just ask for transparency.

Affiliation does not affect inclusion. If the tool meets our criteria, it gets listed regardless of who submitted it.

---

## What Happens After You Contribute

1. A maintainer reviews your submission (typically within 7 days)
2. We verify the company's HQ location, hosting, and product status
3. If approved, it gets merged into the README
4. If rejected, we'll explain why in the issue/PR comments
5. Major contributions may also be added to the scored directory at [eufirst.ai](https://eufirst.ai) with a full sovereignty score and review
